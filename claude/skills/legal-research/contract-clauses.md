# Contract scaffold — CORE pilot agreement

This file lists WHICH clauses a short pilot contract needs and WHAT each must achieve. It is a structure checklist, not validated legal text. Any clause that carries legal weight — liability limitation, statutory references, data-protection roles — must be verified against a live source (or confirmed by a lawyer) before use. Do not present drafted wording as legally sound without that step.

A pilot agreement here is short (1–2 pages). Keep it minimal.

## Clauses a pilot contract should cover

| # | Clause | What it must achieve | Verify before relying on it |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Subject / service | who provides what to whom, scope, term | — |
| 2 | Liability disclaimer | platform is a screening aid, not investment/legal advice; user decides; provider not liable for submitted-data accuracy; liability limited as far as legally permissible | the limit of liability is statute-governed — search what limitation is actually enforceable; do not assert the boundary from memory |
| 3 | Confidentiality / NDA | mutual confidentiality; no disclosure to third parties; survival period | the survival period is a negotiated/legal point — confirm, do not assume a number |
| 4 | Data protection | references a separate processor agreement; assigns controller vs processor roles | the role assignment and the referenced article must be verified live (see dsgvo-checklist.md) |
| 5 | Pilot terms / feedback | free of charge; feedback obligations; provider may end or convert the pilot with notice | — |
| 6 | Term and termination | start/end dates; early-termination notice; data export and deletion after end | deletion timing tied to data-protection rules — verify, do not state a fixed period from memory |
| — | Signatures | place, date, both parties | — |

## Drafting rule

When you draft any of clauses 2, 3, 4, or 6, you are touching legally binding terms. For every statutory reference, deadline, or liability boundary in them, run the loop in SKILL.md (SUCHE → open source → GEPRÜFT with verbatim quote), or mark it UNGEPRÜFT and route to a lawyer. Do not produce confident-looking boilerplate that the user might sign believing it is verified.

## Pre-signature checklist

- [ ] liability disclaimer present and its enforceable limit verified
- [ ] NDA clause present
- [ ] data-protection clause references a verified processor agreement
- [ ] feedback obligation defined
- [ ] term and verified deletion timing set
- [ ] processor agreement signed separately
- [ ] provider DPA (Supabase) confirmed

See the escalation list in SKILL.md. For any first-version contract for paying customers, AT/CH counterparties, a fund's own terms, or amounts above a threshold: lawyer review before signing.
